About TEDx

Ideas worth speading!

TED is a nonprofit that spreads inspiring ideas through short, powerful talks. It started in 1984 as a conference focused on technology, entertainment, and design, but now covers various topics in over 110 languages. TEDx events, organized independently, bring this spirit to local communities worldwide. Passionate individuals curate these events, sharing new ideas and research, sparking conversations within their communities. TEDx events feature live speakers and recorded TED Talks, promoting the mission of "ideas worth spreading." With over 3000 events annually, TEDx continues to inspire and connect people globally.

About TEDxJUET

TEDxJUET is a local, independently organized event at JUET Guna that strives to recreate the unique experience found at TED, where the world's leading thinkers and doers congregate to share what they are most passionate about. know more...
